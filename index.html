Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Daily AI Tool ("we", "our", or "the app") is an automated content creation tool that generates and publishes educational videos about AI tools to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our use of the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • TikTok account data: Username and open ID required to authorize video uploads via the TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Access tokens: OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok, stored securely and used solely for video publishing.
  • Published video metadata: Titles, descriptions, and hashtags of videos posted through our system.

We do not collect personal information from TikTok viewers or followers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on behalf of the account owner
  • Automatically publish AI-generated educational video content to the authorized TikTok account
  • Monitor publishing status via the TikTok API

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

4. TikTok Data

This app integrates with TikTok via the official TikTok Content Posting API. All data handling follows TikTok's platform policies. We only request the video.publish scope required for direct video posting. TikTok's own privacy policy applies to data processed on their platform: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and are never transmitted to external servers beyond TikTok's official API endpoints. Tokens are deleted upon revocation or when the application is uninstalled.

6. Security

All API credentials and tokens are stored in a local .env file on a private machine. We use HTTPS for all communications with TikTok's API. No credentials are stored in version control or shared publicly.

7. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

All videos published through this application are AI-generated. Content is created using automated text generation (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini), text-to-speech synthesis (Kokoro TTS), and stock footage from Pexels. Each video is labeled as AI-generated content in accordance with TikTok's AI content disclosure policy.

8. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the app's access to your TikTok account via TikTok's settings
  • Request deletion of any stored tokens by contacting us
